# Vietnamese Coffee Market — AgentWeb Research Pattern

## Problem: English query bias
Searching "top coffee roaster Vietnam SEO blog" (English marketing jargon) returns:
- Western SEO agency case studies (Nguyen Coffee Supply Shopify case)
- General market reports (Mordor Intelligence, 6Wresearch)
- Irrelevant Reddit noise

Does NOT return actual Vietnamese brands ranking on Google Vietnam.

## Fix: Search as a VN consumer
These queries surface the real competitive landscape:

| Query | What it finds |
|---|---|
| "cà phê specialty mua online" | Visty, XLIII Coffee |
| "cà phê rang xây tại nhà" | Highlands, King Coffee, Hello5 |
| "cà phê rang xây specialty online" | 3C Roastery (Shopee), Flusso, Tam Trinh Coffee |

## Key Vietnamese Specialty Coffee Players (as of May 2026)

### Visty (visty.vn)
- WordPress site, online since ~2018
- Has blog ("Specialty Blog"): Pour Over recipes, Cold Brew, Espresso guide, coffee acidity, French Press
- Has "Cà phê Đặc Sản Subscription" (monthly coffee subscription)
- Also sells brewing equipment (V60, Aeropress, phin filters)
- Customer reviews via Cusrev, 4.87/5 product rating
- Positioning: "Lá cờ đầu trên con sóng cà phê Specialty" (pioneer of specialty coffee wave)
- Category pages: /ca-phe-dac-san-visty-vn/, /shop/dung-cu-may-moc/, /blog/

### Every Half (everyhalf.vn)
- Wix site, strong visual brand
- Blog articles: Sudan Rume variety, Global Coffee Awards 2025 (Overall National Winner Vietnam), membership program, sustainability/New Crop 2025
- Has loyalty/membership program but **no subscription delivery model**
- Products: roasted beans (125g/250g), specialty drip bags (15k-24k VND), merch
- PR coverage: Vietcetera, World Coffee Portal, Saigoneer
- Has physical stores (6E Tú Xương, Nguyễn Văn Thủ, Võ Thị Sáu)
- Focus: farmer storytelling, sustainability, Fine Robusta, Starmaya/Liberica varieties

### XLIII Coffee (xliiicoffee.com / 43factory.coffee)
- Based in Đà Nẵng, rebranded from 43 Factory Coffee Roaster
- Has **two subscription models**:
  1. **Discovery Subscription**: 2 boxes × 250g coffee, delivered 5th each month, min 1 month, free shipping VN
  2. **Hunter Subscription**: 950,000 VND = 10 credits, no expiry, no daily limit, 1-6 credits/coffee by rarity
- Blog: subscription explanations, decaf market trends, Da Nang food guides, vegan lifestyle
- Direct Trade sourcing, transparent traceability
- Origin storytelling focus

### MEE FEE (meefee.vn)
- Newer (registered 2024), Haravan e-commerce platform
- Blog structure: Cà phê 101 (Know Your Joe), Làm thế nào để (How to), Nghệ thuật rang xay (Art of Roasting), Chân dung nông hộ (Farmer Portraits)
- Has dedicated subscription page with individual/business tiers
- Active social (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)

## SEO Content Categories That Work

1. **Educational / "Cà phê 101"** — "Cà phê Liberica là gì?", "Starmaya là gì?", "Giải mã vị ngọt Honey Process"
2. **How-to brewing guides** — "Cách pha Pour Over của James Hoffmann", "Cách pha cà phê phin", "Cold Brew là gì?"
3. **Origin stories / traceability** — Farmer portraits, growing region deep-dives, processing method explainers
4. **B2B / Export targeting** — "Best Vietnamese coffee beans for roasters in UK", price updates
5. **Cultural content** — "Đi cà phê hay đi uống cà phê" (cultural language piece)

## Why This Content Performs
- Low competition: most VN roasters haven't invested in SEO yet
- International demand for VN coffee knowledge is growing (UK, EU, US roasters sourcing from VN)
- Educational content builds authority AND converts to subscription/sales
- Vietnamese-language SEO has even less competition than English
